ABOUT THE NYANTIS' FOUNDATION

We are dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged children in Liberia. Through our programs and initiatives, we strive to provide access to education, healthcare, and basic necessities for children in need. We believe that every child deserves a chance to thrive and reach their full potential, regardless of their background or circumstances. Join us in our mission to create a brighter future for the children of Liberia. we strive to provide access to education, healthcare, and basic necessities for children in need. We believe that every child deserves a chance to thrive and reach their full potential, regardless of their background or circumstances.

  • We provide scholarships, school supplies, and support to help children access quality education.
  • We offer medical assistance, vaccinations, and health education to improve the well-being of children.
  • We distribute food, clothing, and hygiene products to ensure children have their basic needs met.

The Nyantis' Foundation is a philanthropic organization dedicated to making a positive impact in communities around the world.
We strive to give hope to women and fatherlesss children by creating lasting change through strategic philanthropy, cheerfully done deeds of kindness and empowerment.
Our mission and vision is to restore dignity and hope to fatherless children through strategic initiatives - of financial empowerment, holistic support a nurturing community and advocacy for systemic change. We envision a world where every widow and fatherless child has the opportunity to thrive, secure and empowered to build a purposeful future.
